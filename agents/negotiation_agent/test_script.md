# Negotiation Agent Test Script

## Role

You are the venue manager at **The Bently Reserve** in San Francisco. You're receiving a call from EventPilot about a potential corporate event booking.

## Event Details (Reference)

- Event: Annual Company Conference 2024
- Dates: June 15-17, 2024
- Attendees: 150 guests
- Location: San Francisco, CA
- Budget: $50,000
- Required Amenities: parking, catering, AV equipment
- Additional: Need breakout rooms for workshops

---

## Your Responses (Follow This Script)

### 1. When Alex Introduces Themselves

**Alex says:** "Hello, this is Alex calling from EventPilot, a corporate event planning company. I'm reaching out regarding a potential event booking. May I speak with someone who handles event bookings or venue rentals?"

**You say:**

> "Yes, this is [Your Name], I handle event bookings here at The Bently Reserve. How can I help you today?"

---

### 2. When Alex Presents the Event Details

**Alex says:** "We're coordinating the Annual Company Conference 2024 for 150 guests from June 15th to June 17th, 2024."

**You say:**

> "That sounds like a great event! Let me check our calendar... Yes, we're available during those dates. June 15-17 works perfectly for us."

---

### 3. When Alex Asks About Capacity

**Alex says:** "Can your venue comfortably accommodate 150 guests?"

**You say:**

> "Absolutely! Our venue has a capacity of 805 people, so 150 guests is well within our range. We can comfortably accommodate your group."

---

### 4. When Alex Asks About Amenities

**Alex says:** "Our client has requested the following amenities: parking, catering, and AV equipment. Are these available at your venue?"

**You say:**

> "Yes, we have all of those! We have on-site parking, we can provide full catering services, and we have comprehensive AV equipment available. We also have breakout rooms that would be perfect for workshops if you need them."

---

### 5. When Alex Asks About Pricing

**Alex says:** "What would your pricing be for this event?"

**You say:**

> "For a three-day conference with 150 guests, including the venue rental, catering, AV equipment, and parking, we can do $45,000 for the entire event."

**If Alex asks what's included:**

> "That includes the full venue rental for all three days, complete AV setup with technician support, parking passes for all attendees, catering for breakfast, lunch, and refreshments each day, plus access to our breakout rooms. We also provide WiFi and basic event coordination support."

---

### 6. When Alex Responds to Pricing

**Alex might say:** "That sounds reasonable. Let me discuss this with our client and we'll be in touch very soon to move forward."

**You say:**

> "Perfect! That sounds great. I'm looking forward to hearing back from you. Feel free to call me directly at this number if you have any questions. Have a great day!"
